I will also keep my facilitator informed if I find myself falling behind in course work or am feeling overwhelmed and need support. I'm aware that I struggle to ask for help. I battle this by giving myself strict deadlines of asking for help. To help mitigate feeling overwhelmed because I've missed or skipped over important information I keep visual reminders to remain calm, read the course content carefully and take breaks when I feel like I'm moving too quickly.

I expect the facilitation team to be kind, unbiased and open minded. I haven't encountered anyone that isn't though.
